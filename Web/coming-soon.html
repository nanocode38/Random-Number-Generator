Why the Wait?

Different operating systems have significant differences in underlying architecture, security mechanisms, and user interaction paradigms. To ensure macOS and Linux users get an experience as smooth, stable, and deeply integrated with system features as the Windows version, our engineering team is restructuring core modules layer by layer — from process scheduling to graphics rendering, from file system to sandbox security, every link is undergoing meticulous adaptation and optimization.

We reject the shortcut of "wrapper-based porting" and choose to re-implement each key component natively. This means a longer upfront investment, but it also means you will ultimately receive an application that is truly "born and bred" for its platform — not one that barely runs through a compatibility layer.

Good software is worth waiting for patiently. Thank you for your understanding and support 💙
